## Introduction

Tutti Fruttini ("the App") is a free mobile game that uses advertising to support development. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

---

## Data Collection

### 1. Data We Collect Directly

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information directly.** The App:
- Does NOT require user registration or login
- Does NOT collect names, email addresses, or contact information
- Does NOT access your contacts, photos, or files
- Does NOT track your location

### 2. Data Collected by Third Parties

The App uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

- **Advertising ID**: A unique identifier for advertising purposes
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, screen size
- **IP Address**: Your approximate location (city/country level)
- **App Usage Data**: How you interact with ads (views, clicks)
- **Technical Data**: Crash reports, performance metrics

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policies:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google AdMob Privacy Information](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

## How Data Is Used

### By Tutti Fruttini:
- **Local game saves**: High scores and settings are stored locally on your device only
- **No analytics**: We do not track your gameplay or behavior

### By Google AdMob:
- **Personalized advertising**: Showing relevant ads based on your interests
- **Ad performance**: Measuring ad effectiveness
- **Fraud prevention**: Detecting invalid ad traffic

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage (On Your Device):
- **Game saves**: High scores, shake count, and settings are saved to your device using Godot's user data system
- **Location**: `user://save_data.json` (Android: `/data/data/com.bonsaidotdot.tuttifruttini/files/`)
- **No cloud sync**: Your data is not uploaded to our servers

### Third-Party Storage:
- AdMob data is stored on Google's servers according to their retention policies

---

## Your Privacy Rights

### Opt Out of Personalized Ads

**Android:**
1. Open **Settings** → **Google** → **Ads**
2. Enable **Opt out of Ads Personalization**

**Alternative:**
1. Open **Settings** → **Privacy** → **Ads**
2. Enable **Delete advertising ID**

### Disable Ads Completely
Ads are only shown when you choose to watch a rewarded ad to refill shakes. You can play the game without ever watching ads.

### Delete Your Data
To delete all local game data:
1. **Android**: Settings → Apps → Tutti Fruttini → Storage → Clear Data
2. **Or**: Uninstall the app
